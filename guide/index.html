How to Use Executive Path

Step-by-step guide to managing trips

1

login Login or Register

  1. Open the app — you will be redirected to the Login page.
  2. If you already have an account, enter your Username and Password, then click Sign In.
  3. If you are a new user, click the Register tab, fill in your Name, Username, and Password, then click Register.
  4. After successful login, you will be taken to the Dashboard.

info Admin account: username admin, password admin123 — created automatically on first visit.

2

dashboard Dashboard

The Dashboard gives you a quick overview of all your trips.

  • Quick Stats — see your Active, Completed, and Cancelled trip counts.
  • Travel Calendar — view trips scheduled per day. ! indicates schedule conflicts (multiple trips on the same day).
  • Upcoming Trips — list of your upcoming trips with Edit, Complete, and Cancel buttons.
  • Recent Activity — shows the latest changes to your trips.
3

flight_takeoff Create a New Trip

  1. Go to Trips in the sidebar, or click + New Trip at the bottom of the sidebar.
  2. Fill in the form:
    • Trip Name — give your trip a descriptive name.
    • Requestor — who is requesting the trip.
    • Destination — where are you going.
    • Vehicle — assign a vehicle (e.g. Van 1, Van 2).
    • Driver — assign a driver.
    • Date — pick the trip date.
    • Departure Time — set the time of departure.
    • Purpose — describe the reason for the trip.
  3. Click Submit to save the trip.
  4. The trip is automatically saved and will appear on your Dashboard and Trip Itinerary.
4

list_alt View & Manage Trips

There are two ways to view your trips:

  • Dashboard — see upcoming trips in the "Upcoming Trips" section.
  • Trip Itinerary (click a trip card) — view full details, edit, cancel, or mark as complete.

touch_app Click any trip card on the Dashboard to open the full Trip Itinerary view.

5

edit Edit, Cancel, or Complete a Trip

  • Edit — Click the edit icon on a trip card. The form will open pre-filled. Make your changes and click Submit.
  • Cancel — Click the cancel icon to cancel a trip. A confirmation dialog will appear.
  • Complete — Click the check_circle icon to mark a trip as completed.

info Completed trips cannot be edited or cancelled. The Edit and Cancel buttons are hidden for completed trips.

6

bar_chart View Annual Reports

  1. Go to Reports in the sidebar.
  2. Select a year from the dropdown to see that year's data.
  3. The report shows:
    • Total trips, Active, Completed, and Cancelled counts
    • Monthly trip trend bar chart
    • Status distribution
    • Top destinations ranked by trip count
    • Vehicle usage breakdown
    • Trips per requestor (admin sees all users)
    • Full table of all trips for the selected year
  4. Click Print or the print icon to print or save as PDF.
7

settings Settings — Change Password

  1. Go to Settings in the sidebar.
  2. Under Change Password, enter your current password and new password.
  3. Click Update Password to save.
  4. Your Account Info is displayed in the sidebar card (name and username).
8

admin_panel_settings Admin Features

The admin account has special privileges:

  • See all trips from all users on the Dashboard and Trip Itinerary.
  • Each trip card shows the username of the person who created it.
  • Admin can edit, cancel, or complete any user's trip.
  • Reports page shows trips by requestor across all users.
  • An Admin badge appears next to the admin's name in the user menu.

lock Default admin: admin / admin123

9

dark_mode Dark / Light Mode

Toggle between dark and light themes:

  • Click the dark_mode / light_mode icon in the header (next to the Help button).
  • Your preference is saved automatically and persists across visits.
  • Dark mode uses a dark blue-gray color scheme that is easier on the eyes in low light.
10

tips_and_updates Quick Tips

  • Use the search bar in the header to filter trips on any page.
  • The Travel Calendar on the Dashboard shows a ! when you have overlapping trips.
  • Click a trip card on the Dashboard to see full details in the Trip Itinerary.
  • Use Reports to generate printable year-end summaries for management.
  • All data is saved in your browser — no internet connection is needed after the page loads.
